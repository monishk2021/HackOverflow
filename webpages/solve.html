Beginner's guide to use the provided Steps:

U = Upper Layer clockwise D = Down Layer clockwise F = Front Layer clockwise B = Back Layer clockwise Ui = Upper Layer anti-clockwise Di= Down Layer anti-clockwise Fi = Front Layer anti-clockwise Bi = Back Layer anti-clockwise X = Rotate the entire cube clockwise on R (i.e. on the axis from the right of the cube) Y = Rotate the entire cube clockwise on D (i.e. on the axis from the bottom of the cube) Z = Rotate the entire cube clockwise on F (i.e. on the axis from the front of the cube) M = Turn the middle layer between the left and right sides towards you E = Turn the middle layer between the up and down sides away from you S = Turn the middle layer between the front and back sides clockwise
